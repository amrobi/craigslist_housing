<br>
<br>
This vintage walk-up located at 1234 W. Loyola in Rogers Park features studio apartments in a number of different layouts. <br>
<br>
Each apartment has large closets, and thermal pane windows. Heat, hot water, and cooking gas are FREE here making these apartments a great value. Loyola University's main campus is within easy walking distance. 1234 W. Loyola has a twenty-four hour laundry room and intercom entry. Public transportation is VERY convenient with the CTA Red Line being only one block away. <br>
<br>
Restaurants and shopping opportunities abound (including a new Target). You will find quaint coffee houses, a movie theatre, parks, and art museums all within the immediate neighborhood.<br>
